Motorcycling through North East India

Travel is a great teacher and the lessons are most enriching when learnt from the seat of a motorcycle. Our adventure motorcycle tours not only let you enjoy the thrill of riding but also allow you to learn about the unique cultures of the places you travel through. We run these tours in partnership with Chain Reaction, the best known motorcycle tour organiser in eastern India.

Read more

Ride through the lush green tea plantations of Assam, and forests which house the great one-horned rhinoceros and other rare animals. Take a ferry across the mighty Brahmaputra to Majuli, the largest river island in the world and home to the Hindu Vaishnavite spiritual tradition.

Travel further to the tribal heartland of Arunachal Pradesh where you get an opportunity to meet some of its indigenous tribes. Visit a few Galo villages around Aalo and get a glimpse of their way of life. Ride on to meet the Membas of Menchuka and enjoy the solitude and pristine beauty of the place.

Riding westwards in Arunachal Pradesh, cross the snow covered Sela Pass at a lofty 13,700 feet and on to Tawang where you can visit the 600 year old Tawang Gompa, the second largest Buddhist monastery in Asia. The road you take will be the one that His Holiness The Dalai Lama used to escape to India when China invaded Tibet. The lush green Alpine forests of Arunachal Pradesh will entice you deeper into themselves where you will meet numerous hill tribes whose lifestyle has, by and large, remained unchanged over centuries. By night stay in forest camps or in traditional bamboo houses built on stilts, by day tame the rapids of the Siang river in a raft or a kayak. Walk across cane suspension bridges swinging wildly over roaring rivers.

Ride on to Nagaland and pay your respects to the soldiers who sacrificed their lives building the Stillwell Road and defending Kohima and Imphal from the advancing Japanese Army in World War II. Spend time with fierce, tattooed Konyak warriors who used to be headhunters till not very long ago and listen to their amazing tales from a bygone era. Watch them craft old fashioned flintlock and muzzle loading guns and take a shot, literally, at firing them.

Visit fascinating local markets, drink home brewed rice beer in bamboo mugs, walk forest trails, go game fishing and ride for miles through uninhabited forest trails without encountering another vehicle all day. Let the dust settle on your eyelashes, get caked in mud and get soaked in the rain. After a hard day’s ride, relax in a log hut by a river or lakeside and watch large flocks of migratory birds fly past as you toast yourself by a crackling bonfire and eat a peaceful dinner under a starry sky.

End the trip at a pretty getaway in Meghalaya. Check out some of the unique natural formations of the region – rolling hills, deep gorges, gushing waterfalls, crystal clear rivers, living root bridges and intriguing caves. Participate in the traditional sport of archery with the local Khasi tribe.

Our itineraries are flexible and relaxed so that you have enough time to soak in the varied experiences that the region has to offer. We prefer small group sizes so that we can pay close attention to every rider and logistical detail. Come ride with us through the interiors of the North east and let us show it to you through our eyes.
