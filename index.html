Live Their Stories

Anvayins was born out of the desire to bring these lesser known cultures to the fore and thereby create the right motivation for members of these indigenous communities to preserve and continue their unique lifestyles and cultural practices. The journey has been commenced with identifying culturally rich communities in northeastern India and partnering with them to take the discerning traveler on a magical journey through this alluring land of simple tribal people, extraordinary socio-cultural practices and exquisite natural beauty. More about us

Get a peek into the lives of the natives of North East India

The north-eastern region of India is home to many indigenous cultural communities, each with its unique history, mythology, culture and traditions. By visiting them you not only learn about the fascinating lifestyles of people in this region but also encourage them to take pride in these and thereby preserve them.
