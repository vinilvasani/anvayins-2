Arunachal Pradesh

The Galos of Aalo

The Galos of Arunachal Pradesh live predominantly in the middle eastern part of the state and are among the more populous tribes of the region. There are two major district headquarters in this part of Arunachal Pradesh, Aalo and Besar, which though infamous for their poor road connectivity are great starting points for exploring the surrounding Galo villages.

Nagaland

The Konyaks

Nagaland has 16 major tribes with several sub-tribes each. Though all of them have warrior lineages, none has a reputation for ferocity like the Konyaks of Mon district. The most feared even among their tribal brethren, they are known to have continued their headhunting ways for many years even after all the others had discontinued the practice.

Majuli - More Than Meets the Eye

Visit the Mising tribals who have made Majuli their home ever since they migrated here from Arunachal Pradesh around the 13th century.
