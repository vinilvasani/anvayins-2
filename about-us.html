About Us

India is home to a plethora of indigenous communities and cultures, some well-known and others less so. While the popular ones are able to hold their own and even thrive in a fast-changing world, many of the overlooked ones are struggling to survive in the face of modern influences and a general lack of motivation in the locals to sustain them.

Anvayins was born out of the desire to bring these lesser known cultures to the fore and thereby create the right motivation for members of these indigenous communities to preserve and continue their unique lifestyles and cultural practices. The journey has been commenced with identifying culturally rich communities in northeastern India and partnering with them to take the discerning traveler on a magical journey through this alluring land of simple tribal people, extraordinary socio-cultural practices and exquisite natural beauty.

Anvayins is committed to facilitating and, wherever required, creating appropriate infrastructure for travelers to live the stories of the places they visit without compromising on comfort.

The Team

Nishikant Das (Nishi), the brain behind Anvayins, is an ex-investment banker with more than 20 years’ experience in the financial services industry across organisations such as HDFC Bank, Standard Chartered Bank and CRISIL. An engineer from IIT Kanpur and MBA from XIM Bhubaneswar, Nishi’s passion for experiencing cultures and lifestyles in remote Indian regions has taken him across the length and breadth of the country, the secluded north-eastern Himalayan region being his personal favourite. Nishi highly values the bonds he forms with local communities while on these trips.

He found an ally in Shahwar Hussain on one such foray into the North East.  Owner of Chain Reaction, the best known motorcycling company in the North East, Shahwar’s understanding of this region goes beyond the ordinary. With his roots in Assam–one of the 7 sisters–Shahwar is familiar with most of the local tongues. Add to this the fact that he is a published travel writer and automotive journalist, and you will find only a handful of people who can match his expertise and passion for showcasing the uniqueness of this fascinating corner of India.

With complementary skills and a shared vision, the duo focuses its combined energy on its common passion–seeking fascinating experiences in remote Indian regions and sharing them with like-minded individuals in a socially responsible and ecologically sensitive way, while ensuring that comfort is key to the experience.
