Menchuka is inhabited primarily by the Memba tribals—Buddhists of Mongoloid stock and a particularly genial people. They have lived here for centuries, as a result of which they can boast of a rich cultural and mythological heritage. Menchuka itself is believed to have been blessed into existence by Guru Rinpoche, the 8th century saint who is revered by many as the ‘second Buddha’
